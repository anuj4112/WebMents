## 1. Getting Started

### System Requirements

**Minimum Requirements:**
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- Screen resolution: 1024x768 or higher

**Recommended:**
- Latest version of Chrome or Firefox
- Stable broadband connection
- Screen resolution: 1920x1080

### Accessing WebMents

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to: `http://localhost:3000` (or your deployed URL)
3. You'll see the landing page with options to Login or Sign Up

## 2. For Manufacturers

### 2.1 Registration

**Step 1: Navigate to Signup**
- Click "Get Started" or "Signup" button on the landing page

**Step 2: Select Role**
- Click on the "Manufacturer" card (🏭 icon)
- The card will highlight in purple when selected

**Step 3: Fill in Details**
- **Business Name**: Enter your company/business name
- **Email**: Enter a valid business email address
- **Password**: Create a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
- **City**: Enter your business location
- **Logo**: Upload your company logo (optional, but recommended)
  - Supported formats: JPG, PNG
  - Maximum size: 5MB
  - Recommended: Square image, 500x500px

**Step 4: Submit**
- Click "Create Account"
- Wait for confirmation message
- You'll be redirected to the login page

### 2.2 Login

1. Enter your registered email
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Login"
4. You'll be redirected to the Manufacturer Dashboard

### 2.3 Manufacturer Dashboard Overview

**Dashboard Sections:**

1. **Statistics Cards** (Top)
   - Total Products: Number of products in your catalogue
   - Total Orders: Number of orders received
   - Rating: Your average rating
   - Profile Views: Number of times your profile was viewed

2. **Add/Edit Product Form** (Middle)
   - Form to add new products or edit existing ones

3. **Your Products** (Bottom)
   - Grid view of all your products

### 2.4 Adding Products

**Step 1: Fill Product Details**
- **Product Name**: Enter descriptive product name
  - Example: "Premium Cotton T-Shirt"
- **Price**: Enter price in rupees (₹)
  - Example: 250
- **Category**: Enter product category
  - Example: "T-Shirts", "Jeans", "Shirts"
- **Product Image**: Upload clear product image
  - Use well-lit, high-quality images
  - Show product from multiple angles if possible

**Step 2: Save Product**
- Click "Save Product" button
- Wait for success message
- Product will appear in "Your Products" section

**Best Practices:**
- Use clear, descriptive product names
- Set competitive prices
- Choose appropriate categories
- Upload high-quality images
- Keep product information accurate

### 2.5 Editing Products

**Step 1: Locate Product**
- Scroll to "Your Products" section
- Find the product you want to edit

**Step 2: Click Edit**
- Click the "Edit" button on the product card
- Form will populate with existing data
- You'll be scrolled to the form section

**Step 3: Make Changes**
- Update product name, price, or category
- Note: Image cannot be changed during edit (delete and re-add if needed)

**Step 4: Update**
- Click "Update Product"
- Wait for confirmation
- Changes will reflect immediately

### 2.6 Deleting Products

**Step 1: Locate Product**
- Find the product in "Your Products" section

**Step 2: Delete**
- Click the "Delete" button
- Confirm deletion in the popup dialog
- Product will be removed immediately

**Warning:** Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone!

### 2.7 Managing Orders

**Viewing Orders:**
- Orders appear in the "Total Orders" statistic
- Future updates will include detailed order management

**Order Information:**
- Buyer details
- Products ordered
- Quantities
- Total amount
- Order status

### 2.8 Profile Management

**Updating Profile:**
- Currently, profile updates require contacting support
- Future updates will include profile editing

**Logo Update:**
- To change logo, contact support with new logo file

## 3. For Buyers

### 3.1 Registration

**Step 1: Navigate to Signup**
- Click "Get Started" or "Signup" button

**Step 2: Select Role**
- Click on the "Buyer" card (🛒 icon)

**Step 3: Fill in Details**
- **Full Name**: Enter your name or business name
- **Email**: Enter valid email address
- **Password**: Create strong password
- **City**: Enter your location
- **Profile Picture**: Upload photo (optional)

**Step 4: Submit**
- Click "Create Account"
- Redirected to login page after success

### 3.2 Login

1. Enter registered email
2. Enter password
3. Click "Login"
4. Redirected to Buyer Dashboard

### 3.3 Buyer Dashboard Overview

**Dashboard Sections:**

1. **Search Section** (Top)
   - Search by city
   - Search products

2. **Manufacturers Section** (Middle)
   - Grid of all registered manufacturers
   - Filter by location

3. **All Products Section** (Bottom)
   - Grid of all available products
   - Search and filter capabilities

### 3.4 Searching Manufacturers

**By City:**
1. Click on "Search by city" input
2. Type city name (e.g., "Mumbai")
3. Results filter automatically as you type
4. Clear search to see all manufacturers

**Viewing Manufacturer Profile:**
- Click on any manufacturer card
- Or click "View Catalogue" button
- Opens manufacturer's complete catalogue

### 3.5 Browsing Products

**View All Products:**
- Scroll to "All Products" section
- Browse through product grid
- Click any product for details

**Search Products:**
1. Use "Search products" input
2. Type product name or category
3. Results filter in real-time
4. Search works across all manufacturers

**Product Information:**
- Product image
- Product name
- Price in rupees (₹)
- Category

### 3.6 Viewing Catalogues

**Step 1: Select Manufacturer**
- Click on manufacturer card or "View Catalogue" button

**Step 2: Browse Catalogue**
- View all products from that manufacturer
- See product details
- Compare prices

**Step 3: Contact Manufacturer**
- Note manufacturer's contact information
- Reach out for bulk orders
- Discuss requirements

### 3.7 Placing Orders

**Current Process:**
1. Browse products
2. Note down products of interest
3. Contact manufacturer directly
4. Discuss quantities and pricing
5. Finalize order details

**Future Enhancement:**
- In-app order placement
- Shopping cart functionality
- Direct payment integration

### 3.8 Comparing Products

**Manual Comparison:**
1. Open multiple manufacturer catalogues in different tabs
2. Compare prices, quality, and terms
3. Make informed decisions

**Tips:**
- Compare similar products across manufacturers
- Consider location for shipping costs
- Check manufacturer ratings
- Read product descriptions carefully

## 4. Common Features

**Logout:**

- Session persists until logout

**Security:**
- Always logout on shared computers
- Don't share login credentials
- Use strong passwords

### 4.3 Image Uploads

**Supported Formats:**
- JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
- PNG (.png)

**Size Limits:**
- Maximum: 5MB per image
- Recommended: Under 2MB for faster uploads

**Image Guidelines:**
- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Avoid blurry images
- Show product clearly
- Use neutral backgrounds
- Square images work best

### 4.4 Search Functionality

**Real-time Search:**
- Results update as you type
- No need to press Enter
- Case-insensitive search

**Search Tips:**
- Use specific keywords
- Try different spellings
- Use category names
- Clear search to reset

## 5. Troubleshooting

### 5.1 Login Issues

**Problem: "Invalid credentials" error**

**Solutions:**
1. Check email spelling
2. Verify password (case-sensitive)
3. Ensure you're registered
4. Try password reset (if available)

**Problem: Can't access dashboard after login**

**Solutions:**
1. Clear browser cache
2. Try different browser
3. Check internet connection
4. Contact support

### 5.2 Upload Issues

**Problem: Image won't upload**

**Solutions:**
1. Check file size (max 5MB)
2. Verify file format (JPG/PNG only)
3. Try different image
4. Check internet connection
5. Refresh page and try again

**Problem: "Error adding product"**

**Solutions:**
1. Fill all required fields
2. Check image is selected
3. Verify price is a number
4. Try again after refreshing

### 5.3 Display Issues

**Problem: Products not showing**

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Check internet connection
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser

**Problem: Images not loading**

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Wait for images to load
3. Refresh the page
4. Clear browser cache

### 5.4 Search Issues

**Problem: Search returns no results**

**Solutions:**
1. Check spelling
2. Try broader search terms
3. Clear search and try again
4. Verify data exists

## 6. FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is WebMents free to use?**
A: Yes, the platform is currently free for all users.

**Q: Can I register as both manufacturer and buyer?**
A: You need separate accounts for each role. Use different email addresses.

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Password change feature is coming soon. Contact support for now.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: We take security seriously. However, always use strong passwords and logout on shared devices.

### For Manufacturers

**Q: How many products can I add?**
A: There's no limit on the number of products you can add.

**Q: Can I edit product images?**
A: Currently, you need to delete and re-add the product to change images.

**Q: How do buyers contact me?**
A: Buyers can see your email and contact you directly.

**Q: Can I see who viewed my products?**
A: Detailed analytics are coming in future updates.

**Q: How do I get verified?**
A: Verification process will be implemented soon. All manufacturers are currently unverified.

### For Buyers

**Q: How do I place an order?**
A: Currently, contact manufacturers directly. In-app ordering is coming soon.

**Q: Can I save favorite manufacturers?**
A: This feature is planned for future updates.

**Q: How do I compare products?**
A: Open multiple catalogues in different browser tabs for comparison.

**Q: Are prices negotiable?**
A: Contact manufacturers directly to discuss pricing for bulk orders.

**Q: Can I see manufacturer reviews?**
A: Review system is planned for future implementation.
